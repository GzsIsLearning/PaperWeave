> **Abstract:** Comprehensive survey tracing RS foundation models from unimodal to multimodal. Systematically categorizes models, provides tutorial-style guidance for beginners, and discusses key challenges. Covers models from RingMo to SkySense and beyond. Answers: What, Why, and How of RS FMs.

1. **Evolutionary perspective:** Traces RS FMs from single-modality (RingMo, SatMAE) through multi-sensor (SkySense, CROMA) to unified multi-modal models.
2. **Tutorial focus:** Practical guidance on fine-tuning, adapting, and deploying RS FMs — useful for practitioners.
3. **Five key model categories identified:** Unimodal optical, unimodal SAR, unimodal spectral, multimodal contrastive, multimodal generative.
